AND Digital / Join

Join a Club, and be part of something remarkable.

AND is on a mission to close the world’s skill gap by accelerating the digital capabilities of ambitious organisations. To do this, we need thinkers, tinkerers, passionate software craftspeople and inspiring technologists to help us solve these challenges.

Drive impact faster

When you join AND, you’ll be part of a Club of 100 ANDis who you’ll learn, work and enjoy downtime with - so you retain a network of peers, regardless of how big we get or how fast we grow.

Since each Club works with a focused number of clients, this often means you can achieve visible impact with clients far faster than it's possible to achieve in larger organisations. That could involve transforming the way they deliver products to building a brand new app for a fast-growing startup; creating a website for a large household name, or developing a platform for internal use.

Get set for success

Our award-winning Bootcamp is the perfect way to start your career here at AND. Three weeks of icebreakers and workshops, and your first build project, ensure you have everything you need to hit the ground running when you join your squad.

Develop, as you develop

Beyond Bootcamp, we’re committed to investing long-term in our people. We provide a variety of initiatives and opportunities to support your development, right from the get go.

  • Embark on a 3-month plan, focusing on your strengths and development.

  • Join learning days, training and more, with partners like AWS and Microsoft.

  • Enjoy peer-to-peer learning and share knowledge, with our Communities of Practice, extensive learning catalogue and workshops.

  • Progress your career, with training budgets, individual career coaches and our 12-month Personal Growth Programme.

Perks of the job

We also understand that it's not all about work. That’s why we support and recognise our ANDis with a range of benefits, designed to bring a good balance to your working life.

Here’s a (very) small snapshot of the perks available to all our ANDis:

  • Get creative with 10 upskilling and 3 innovation days per year.

  • Give time to discuss causes that matter, with 2 volunteering days a year.

  • Enjoy £500 each year to put toward tech of your choice.

  • Earn extra, by referring future ANDis and clients.

  • Access a 24/7 Employee Assistance Programme.

  • Stay healthy with our dental and healthcare scheme.

  • Support your family with childcare vouchers.

  • Extend your leave when needed, with flexible holidays.
