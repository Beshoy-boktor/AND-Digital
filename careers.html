Early Careers At AND

Would like to meet

We’re always on the lookout for fresh talent, and that includes talented grads looking to build a career in tech. Our ideal graduate looks a little like this:

  • You’ve completed a course in coding (it doesn’t have to be a degree), and can code in at least one language.

  • You’re passionate about the way tech and digital products are changing the world, hungry to learn, and you’re always up-to-date with new technologies.

  • You’re keen to build a well-rounded career that focuses on people and client relationships, as well as building great digital solutions.

We’re committed to giving you the freedom and flexibility to find your feet, explore your career options and work on a range of clients and projects. Take a look below to learn more.

Focus on your foundations

At the beginning of your career in digital, it's important to think about the foundations you're laying: are you building the right connections that may help you in the future, and getting exposure to a variety of experiences?

We believe the network you build in your career is vital: after all, who knows what opportunities it might lead to? When you join us, you aren’t part of a faceless grad intake. You’re part of a Club - literally, becoming a key part of a 100-strong network of ANDis. You’ll build important relationships in this close-knit environment where your achievements and impact are highly visible, which can really help to accelerate your progression.

One of the other great advantages of starting your career at AND is the diversity of our work. You’ll get to hone your tech and product skills, gaining invaluable experiences across a wide range of projects. This gives you a solid foundation for your future career in tech.

Hit the ground running

When you start at AND, you join one of our award-winning Bootcamps, and spend three weeks learning everything from our values and culture, to Agile methodologies and Scrum Master training.

You’ll also work in small teams for two five-day sprints on your first Build project: it’s like an internal hackathon, where you’ll work together to solve a particular brief.

The Bootcamp ends with a presentation of what you have achieved during the Build project, after which you’ll head off to celebrate the end of your first three weeks at AND.

Drive your own learning

Your learning doesn’t stop after leaving the lecture theatre or the classroom. At AND, you can make the most of a range of learning opportunities, including:

  • A career coach, on hand to help you find your own path.

  • Dedicated training budgets and days to refine your skills.

  • An extensive learning catalogue, filled to the brim with resources and workshops.

How to join us

The recruitment process for our graduates is straightforward, with five key steps:

  1. STAND OUT

    Send our dedicated grad recruitment team your CV and a cover letter, telling us why you stand out from the crowd.

  2. LET'S TALK

    If we see a potential fit after reading through your application, one of our recruiters will be in touch for an initial chat as quick as possible.

  3. TECH TEST

    To get a sense of where you’re at, skillswise, we next send you an online test to complete in your own time. Take your time and, when you’re ready, send it back over for review.

  4. FACE TIME

    It’s time to join one of our assessment centres. Here, you’ll take part in a group exercise and have the opportunity to spend some time with an ANDi in a similar role.

  5. JOIN THE CLUB

    If it’s a thumbs up, you’ll be on your way to joining the AND team. And, from the moment you say yes, we’ll be there to make sure you feel part of the ANDi family.

An internship that counts

We hear you. The first year of uni is done, and already that question is getting asked by everyone from your mum to great uncle Gerald: “So, what are you going to do after university?”

Finding the right internship while you are studying can really help you find the answer. But more often that not, an internship involves a lot of shadowing, and not much hands-on learning.

Internships at AND are different. We offer real exposure to the industry, through a 12-month placement for penultimate year students. On this intensive programme, you will:

  • Work on active projects with real clients.

  • Benefit from a wide schedule of training courses that we run every week, alongside other ANDis from across the organisation.

  • Be part of a close-knit team, with support from your squad lead and peers.

  • Be taught languages and technologies you haven’t yet been exposed to at uni.

And, at the end, you have the opportunity to join AND as a full-time graduate on completion of your studies.
