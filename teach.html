Coaching and award-winning learning experiences to ensure your teams are ready for anything.

We work with organisations to build great digital teams that are ready for anything.

Whether you plan to launch a new product proposition, embark on a transformation or deliver a complex replatforming project, your success depends on more than the tech you choose: it depends on your people.

Today, that means not only setting them up to win here and now but enabling them to win in the future. However, the rapid pace of technology and the widening digital skills gap means that’s no easy feat: companies are struggling to deliver whilst at the same hire, onboard and continuously upskill, their digital teams.

And that’s where we come in.

What we offer

Learning and development at AND is driven by our award-winning Academy specialists and our in-house team of dedicated Agile coaches. Together, they enable us to continuously upskill our own ANDis and provide learning experiences tailored to the needs of individual client teams.

  1. ONBOARDING

    New starter experiences that equip individuals with everything they need to hit the ground running - informed by our own award-winning AND Bootcamp.

  2. COACHING

    Dedicated Agile coaching for teams - regardless of level or Agile maturity. Whether Scrum or Kanban, SAFe or XP, our primary aim is to foster high performance, resilience and responsiveness to change.

  3. MENTORING

    Bespoke learning pathways that provide the necessary structure and momentum to drive forward individual personal development and create remarkable digital leaders.

  4. EVENTS

    Workshops and talks, hackathons and meetups - we design and host a wide range of events to reinforce learning experiences and build thriving digital communities. Join us.

  5. CLASSROOM

    Access to an extensive learning catalogue, with hundreds of sessions across Agile, tech, product and professional skills. Designed by our expert ANDis, dedicated coaches and trusted partners.

The benefits

We're big believers in the idea that experience is the best teacher.

Pragmatic and flexible, we’re not much for preaching. We understand the pressures of business today and the complexities of implementing Agile - regardless of your company’s size. Being practioner-led means the learning programmes we create reflect this: expect honest lessons and practical knowledge, with real tales from the trenches and a proven understanding of what does - and doesn’t - work.

  1. EXPAND YOUR CAPABILITY

    Scale and enhance the skills, practices and behaviours you need to enable successful in-house digital delivery.

  2. BUILD COLLABORATIVE TEAMS

    Foster high performance and better outcomes with collaborative team delivery practices and collective ownership.

  3. DELIVER CONSISTENT WoW

    Ensure consistency and rigour across delivery and your ways of working (WoW) with practical Agile know-how.

  4. OPTIMISE YOUR DELIVERY

    Set yourself up to win with guidance and support on team set-up, tools and the correct methodologies to apply.
